About Me

I have been a creative professional for over twelve years. My education as an architect provided me with a strategic thought process that has opened many doors into the design industry, ranging from architectural and furniture design to photography to graphic and interactive design. What excites me is the art of programming when it is combined with the art of design. I love learning and want to always be teachable. I build stand alone micro-sites or collaborate with others on large projects. I'm willing and capable of quickly learning any language and technology required to craft an optimal solution. My colleagues describe me as energetic and conscientious. I am a highly self-motivated person who constantly strives to be the best at what I am doing and will endlessly raise the bar for any challenge put in front of me. I find that solving new programming challenges or learning new languages and frameworks gives me a spark of excitement that naturally motivates me to keep pushing myself further. My competencies are front and back-end JavaScript development, customer service, and consulting. I have experience in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript, jQuery, Node.js, Express, Google Firebase, MySQL, GitHub, Heroku, RESTful APIs, JSON and AJAX.
